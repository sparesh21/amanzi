About Us

Amanzi Sky Deck & Bar, our name is what we strive to be. When you walk through the doors, you enter a luxurious atmosphere where your senses come alive with the very best in food, wine, hookah and a magnificent sky deck. We're hidden off the main street of beautiful hill road, Bandra. A place both familiar and unlike anywhere else, thrives in serving you scrumptious food, steeped to order just for you, host to music & pool parties, and much more.

Come experience our chic décor of blue, yellow and white hues. Intimate sittings that lend an air of opulence during the day and a magical glow from flickering poolside at night. We can also host your next pool party alongside some hookah and of course, great music!
